Who We Are

About ICC Mongolia

The International Chamber of Commerce Mongolia is the national committee of the world's largest business organisation, representing over 45 million companies in more than 170 countries.

We advocate for business interests, facilitate international trade, and provide dispute resolution services to promote sustainable economic development in Mongolia.

Our mission is to create a favorable business environment that enables Mongolian enterprises to thrive in the global marketplace through strong governance, open markets, and responsible business conduct.
